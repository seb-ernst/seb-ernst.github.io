I am an Economics PhD candidate at the London School of Economics. My research interests are in public economics, labour economics and industrial organisation.

Publications

Risk Scores for Long-Term Unemployment and the Assignment to Job Search Counseling with Andreas I. Mueller and Johannes Spinnewijn, AEA Papers and Proceedings, May 2024

Replication Package
This paper studies how risk scores can be used to better allocate job seekers to counseling programs, improving long-term employment outcomes. Using administrative data, we evaluate assignment mechanisms and simulate counterfactuals under alternative targeting rules.
